District Montreal

District Montreal is an iPhone app that allows you to explore underground art, green spaces and audio tours in Montreal.

BCA Research

BCA Research is an iPad app which allows BCA's customers to download, read and annotate publications they subscribe to. Users can also search for publications and listen to webcasts.

Thirdshelf is an iPad app which allows users to browse items in-store and share things they like with their friends.

Status Quotes

Status Quotes is an iPhone app that allows users to relive past status updates their friends have made on Facebook.

Organik

Hypnorganic

Hypnorganic is an interactive exploratory visual project written in javascript inspired by life on earth and creates a (simple) simulation of how beings come to exist and fade over time.
